About Us

CHURCH HISTORY

Christian Covenant Compass was officially established in 2015 by Pastor Stella. It started in a single room with just five members, including two of her children. Despite having limited resources and facing a hostile environment, Pastor Stella's perseverance, hard work, and prayers, along with the support of Apostle Juma, have transformed the church. It now has a large hall that can accommodate over 70 members, along with a kitchen, office, storage, restrooms, and a spacious compound. The community's attitude has also become more supportive, recognizing the positive impact the church has made.

Our Vision

VISION

Our vision is to raise and transform individuals who will continue Christ's legacy for generations to come: John 4:7-10. When a Samaritan woman came to draw water, Jesus said to her, "Will you give me a drink?" His disciples had gone into the town to buy food. The Samaritan woman said to him, "You are a Jew and I am a Samaritan woman. How can you ask me for a drink?" For Jews do not associate with Samaritans. Jesus answered her, "If you knew the gift of God and who it is that asks you for a drink, you would have asked him and he would have given you living water."
